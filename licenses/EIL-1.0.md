### 2. Environmental Impact Assessment

Licensees must:

a) **Conduct Impact Assessments**: Regularly evaluate the environmental impact of software deployment, including energy usage, resource consumption, and electronic waste generation.

b) **Lifecycle Analysis**: Consider the full lifecycle environmental cost from development through deployment to decommissioning.

c) **Baseline Metrics**: Establish baseline environmental metrics and set improvement targets.

### 3. Mitigation Requirements

You must implement measures to:

a) **Optimize Resource Usage**: Minimize CPU, memory, storage, and network resource consumption.

b) **Green Infrastructure**: Prioritize deployment on certified green data centers and cloud providers with strong environmental commitments.

c) **Efficient Algorithms**: Use algorithms and data structures that minimize computational complexity and resource requirements.

d) **Hardware Longevity**: Design software to run efficiently on older hardware to extend device lifecycles and reduce e-waste.

### 4. Transparency and Reporting

For significant deployments, licensees should:
- Publish annual environmental impact reports
- Share methodologies for measuring impact
- Disclose carbon offset initiatives
